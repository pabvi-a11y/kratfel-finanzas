Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

KRATFEL Finanzas ("the App") is a private, internal financial dashboard operated by Naval Solutions, LLC ("we", "us"). The App is used exclusively by a small number of authorized employees of the company to view the company's own financial reserve and cash-flow information. The App is not offered to the public.

1. Information we access

With your authorization, the App connects to QuickBooks Online through Intuit's official API and reads the company's own accounting data, such as transactions, accounts, and categories. The App also stores reserve-account balances that authorized users enter manually. The App requests read-only access; it does not create, modify, or delete data in QuickBooks Online.

2. How we use the information

The information is used solely to display financial summaries, profit-and-loss breakdowns, and reserve runway forecasts to authorized users within the company. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with any third party, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. How we store and protect it

Data is stored on our hosting provider (SiteGround) in a private database. Access tokens for QuickBooks Online are encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM). All access to the App requires authentication, is restricted to authorized company users, and is served exclusively over HTTPS. Application secrets are kept outside the source-code repository.

4. Data retention

Financial data is retained only as long as needed for the company's internal financial monitoring. Authorized users may disconnect the QuickBooks Online connection at any time, which stops further data retrieval; stored tokens are then revoked and deleted.

5. Third-party services

The App relies on Intuit QuickBooks Online (data source) and SiteGround (hosting). Use of QuickBooks Online is also governed by Intuit's own privacy policy and terms.
